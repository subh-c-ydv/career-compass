# The Mercedes Model — Framework Reference

## Overview

The Mercedes Model is a career coaching framework that maps professional identity across three interconnected domains, visualised as the three points of the Mercedes-Benz star. The **hub** — the centre — represents the personal brand that holds all three points together.

## The Three Points

| Point | Represents | Question it answers |
|---|---|---|
| A | Current Core | Where do I operate today? |
| B | Aspirational Direction | Where am I heading? |
| C | Methodological Identity | How do I work / what's my craft? |

## The Hub

The hub is the personal brand: the through-line narrative that makes sense of all three points simultaneously. A weak hub means the three domains feel like three separate careers. A strong hub means they feel like three facets of one coherent professional.

## Scoring (1–10)

- **Coherence Score** — overall cross-domain narrative fit
- **Hub Strength** — how clear and compelling the personal brand is
- **Domain Scores** — depth and credibility in each individual point
